Welcome to Morni Hills

Morni Hills is a beautiful hill station located in the Panchkula district of the Indian state of Haryana..

It is situated at an altitude of 1,220 meters above sea level and is known for its serene and picturesque landscapes. The lush greenery, the misty mountains, and the fresh air make Morni Hills a perfect destination for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts. It is also famous for its trekking trails, boating, and camping facilities. The tranquility and serenity of this place make it an ideal getaway from the hustle and bustle of city life.

Tourist attractions in Morni Hills

    Morni Fort

    This fort is located on a hilltop and offers a panoramic view of the surrounding area. It is a popular spot for trekking and camping.

    Tikkar Taal

    This is a small lake located in the foothills of the Shivalik range. It is a popular spot for picnics and boating.

    Gurudwara Nada Sahib

    his is a historical Sikh shrine located on the banks of the Ghaggar River. It is an important pilgrimage site for Sikhs.

    Mornihill Wildlife Sanctuary

    his sanctuary is home to a variety of wildlife such as leopards, Indian hare, sambhar deer, and langurs. It is a popular spot for birdwatching and nature walks.
